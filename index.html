About the Project:

This tool is intended for migrants and their family members. It provides guidance on how and why immigration officials may collect DNA samples from migrants. Please use the buttons at the bottom of the page to select the situation that most resembles your own.

This tool is not intended to provide legal advice. Information on this site is for general informational purposes only.
